About

My life is an interesting journey so far!
I was born and raised in South Korea and was very fortunate to move to United States in 2008.
I have graduated middle school and high school in the state of Washington and was able to pursue a major in business and management in University of Washington.
As a Korean citizen, I have done 21 months of mandatory military service. After my service, I worked at a themepark called Everland that is owned by Samsung.

I'm an outgoing person who loves to hang out and communicate with people. I love giving positive energy and sharing that feeling with my friends and family.
Studying at Michigan has motivated me more than ever that I have grown the passion to learn so I contribute in creating a user-friendly environment for people.
